Prototyping

Web site prototyping, for the few who may be unfamiliar with what it is, is typically the stage a web site enters prior to a sites actual creation.

The prototype is typically the collection of mockups in some cases screenshots which you are typically asked to approve prior to the creation of the live site. In printing or advertising you might call this a proof.


However the potential uses of these prototypes can go far beyond the creation of an actual fully fledged live project. They can act as an invaluable tool in:

  • Market Research
  • Project Planning
  • Raising Funds
  • Even Winning New Clients
They can aide you in ironing out some of the unknowables present in your project for a fraction of the cost. And what’s more once the unknowables are known, the assets already you’ve already invested in are seldom wasted.
