Industries Period Is A Wordpress Site

Phil and I are often to be found sinking a few jars and chatting about off the wall business ideas, marketing strategies motoring etc. He asked me to build this newspaper styled Wordpress site for him as part of a plan he has to target the US market with marketing information.

It leverages someone else's theme, I didn't build the theme, but I made some modifications to the the theme, including the logo, a custom menu area etc. I also bought the domain, configured the hosting and added analytics.

It's an interesting idea, but as you can see he has a fair bit more copy to write.
